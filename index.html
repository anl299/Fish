It's Big Mouth Billy Bass Time!!!

Use the Draw and Erase tools to add/remove segments to control the fishes movement.
Click on the "Head Tail" title or the image below the title to swap between the fishes head and tail.
* The head and tail are controlled by the same motor, so the fish can only move (outward) one of those body parts at a time.
The downward slope seen after each movement shows the amount of time until the springs return said body part to its original location.

Begin by loading or recording some audio.
